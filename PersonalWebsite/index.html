Who I Am

My background is in social science research, with three years of experience designing and administrating independent research projects. My passion for analytic problem-solving drove me towards web development. I have been practicing front-end development through building small projects since June 2014. I have also implemented several Wordpress websites for freelance clients.


I have spent the past few months at Coder Foundry building fully-functioning .Net web applications. My portfolio features these and other projects. I am currently available for full-time and contract work in .Net and front-end application development.
